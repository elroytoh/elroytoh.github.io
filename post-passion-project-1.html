Passion Project #1

23 June 2026

For a long time now, I have been looking at Pokemon Cards as an alternative investment source. I have been steadily buying sealed products & keeping them at home for them to appreciate in price. However, this got me thinking whether it’s possible to create signals to buy/sell them at an appropriate price.

After thinking it through, I decided to build a dashboard that could help provide leading indicators on which cards to buy/sell, it’s liquidity & best sealed products to transact. I hope this can give a target price & hopefully this can lead to a buyer’s marketplace as well.

Let’s see where this goes!
